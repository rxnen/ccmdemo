Better Together

A year-long friendship across generations

Better Together pairs you with a senior citizen in our community for a full year of shared learning, stories, and genuine friendship. It's not a one-off visit and it's not community service you forget by Monday — it's a relationship you'll actually keep.

1️⃣

You get matched

At the start of the year, you're paired one-on-one with a senior partner — the beginning of your own story together.

2️⃣

You meet monthly

Once a month you come together to learn, cook, celebrate holidays, and trade stories — yours and theirs.

3️⃣

You become friends

By spring, your partner isn't a project — they're someone you genuinely look forward to seeing.
